Overview

What LlamaChat Is

LlamaChat is a local AI chat app designed to do its thinking on your device. It is meant to feel personal and private without sending your conversations off to a remote model.

Inside The Fence

The app is built around local inference, local storage, and a secure-by-default posture. It is meant to stay where it belongs.

Llama Personality

The assistant is personable, humble, a little silly, and a little mystical, but it should still act like a grounded local tool.

Usage Guide

How To Use LlamaChat

LlamaChat is a friendly fenced-in llama built to munch grass in peace. It keeps its gates shut, its pasture private, and its wooly thoughts on your device. This app is designed to keep your data secure and local without any snooping. Llamas do not like peekers. It is also meant to feel approachable and kid-friendly while keeping the fence closed tight.

1. Open The Gate

Type a message into the chat box and send it through the fence. The llama answers from inside its own secure pasture using local on-device thinking.

2. Talk To The Llama

If you allow microphone access, you can use voice mode to speak with the llama directly. If you do not, text chat still works just fine and the gates stay just as secure.

3. Tend The Pasture

LlamaChat keeps local memory so conversations can continue over time, but you are the ranch boss. You can clear saved chats or reset the llama profile whenever you want a fresh field.

4. Keep The Fence Strong

Llamas are very aware of privacy and security. Optional settings can improve the experience, but this llama is built to stay local, friendly, and safe even without extra permissions.

Privacy Policy

How Data Is Handled

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Local Processing

LlamaChat is designed so chat inference happens locally on your device rather than through a cloud AI service.

Optional Permissions

Voice and notification permissions may improve the experience, but they are not required for normal text chat.

No Ads

LlamaChat is designed without in-app ads. Purchasing the app is intended to be the only payment required to use it.
